Code of Conduct

1. Ethical Hacking Principles

As members of CyberSec Academy, we commit to:

  • Only perform security testing with explicit permission
  • Never attempt to harm or compromise systems without authorization
  • Report vulnerabilities responsibly through proper channels
  • Respect privacy and confidentiality of others
  • Use knowledge gained for defensive and educational purposes

2. Community Guidelines

Our community thrives on mutual respect and collaboration. We expect members to:

  • Be respectful and professional in all interactions
  • Help others learn and grow in their cybersecurity journey
  • Share knowledge responsibly and ethically
  • Maintain a positive and inclusive environment
  • Report any violations of this code of conduct

3. Academic Integrity

To maintain the value of our certifications and learning paths:

  • Complete challenges and assessments independently
  • Do not share solutions or answers with others
  • Report any cheating or academic dishonesty
  • Take pride in your genuine achievements
  • Help others learn without giving direct answers

4. Consequences of Violations

Violations of this code may result in:

  • Warning or temporary suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Revocation of certifications
  • Legal action if applicable

5. Reporting

If you witness or experience any violations:

  • Report immediately to our moderation team
  • Provide detailed information about the incident
  • Maintain confidentiality of the reporting process
  • Cooperate with any resulting investigations

6. Contact

For questions about this Code of Conduct or to report violations, please contact us at:

Email: conduct@cybersecacademy.com
